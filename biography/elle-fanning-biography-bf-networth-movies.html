Elle Fanning: Biography of a Hollywood Star

Elle Fanning is a famous actress known for her roles in movies and TV shows. She is one of Hollywood’s brightest stars and started her career at a very young age. Let’s learn more about her life and achievements!

Who is Elle Fanning?

Elle Fanning was born on April 9, 1998, in Conyers, Georgia, USA. She is 26 years old (as of 2025). Elle comes from a family of artists; her older sister, Dakota Fanning, is also a well-known actress. Elle followed in her sister's footsteps and became a star herself.

Elle Fanning’s Journey in Acting

Elle Fanning began acting at the age of two, playing the younger version of her sister’s character in the movie “I Am Sam”. She quickly gained recognition for her talent and started getting her own roles in movies and TV shows.

She is famous for her performances in movies like:

  • “Maleficent” (2014) – She played Princess Aurora alongside Angelina Jolie.
  • “The Neon Demon” (2016) – A bold role in a psychological thriller.
  • “Super 8” (2011) – A science-fiction hit directed by J.J. Abrams.
  • “The Great” (2020–Present) – A TV series where Elle stars as Catherine the Great.

Her incredible acting skills have made her a favorite among fans and critics alike.
