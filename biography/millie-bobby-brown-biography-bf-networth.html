Millie Bobby Brown: A Star’s Biography

Millie Bobby Brown is a talented actress, model, and entrepreneur. She became famous for her role in the popular TV show “Stranger Things.” Let’s learn more about her exciting life!

Who is Millie Bobby Brown?

Millie Bobby Brown was born on February 19, 2004, in Marbella, Spain. Her parents are British, and they moved back to England when Millie was very young. She is 21 years old (as of 2025).

Millie grew up with a passion for acting. Even as a little girl, she loved performing and entertaining people. She worked hard and got her first big break with “Stranger Things.”

How Did Millie Bobby Brown Become Famous?

Millie became a superstar when she played the role of Eleven in the Netflix series “Stranger Things.” People loved her performance, and she won many awards. Since then, she has acted in movies like “Enola Holmes” and “Godzilla vs. Kong.”

She is also a producer and helped create the “Enola Holmes” movies. Millie is one of the youngest people in Hollywood to have such success!
